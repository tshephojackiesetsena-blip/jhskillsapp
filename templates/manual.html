Admin Manual

This guide keeps the admin work separate from the student portal so registration, login handover, and support remain organized.

Step 1

Register the Student

Use the student registration page to capture personal details, qualification, modules, emergency contact, and portal access information.

Step 2

Issue Student Credentials

After registration, provide the generated username and password so the student can use the student-only login page.

Step 3

Student Uses Separate Portal

Students log in on /student-login and see their own dashboard, tasks, schedule, fees, notes, support, and records.

Step 4

Upload Required Documents

Admin can open a student profile and upload required records, while students can also manage their own document checklist from their profile page.
